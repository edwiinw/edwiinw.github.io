# Product

## Register

brand

## Platform

web

## Users

Recruiters and design leaders evaluating Edwiin for UI/UX design roles.

## Product Purpose

Present Edwiin’s design thinking, selected project work, professional profile, and contact details in a memorable portfolio.

## Positioning

Edwiin turns complex product problems into precise, considered digital experiences.

## Conversion & proof

- Primary CTA: view complete project case studies.
- Secondary CTA: contact Edwiin by email and review the résumé.
- Memorable line: Thoughtful systems. Clear experiences.
- Proof on hand: selected color-system and brand-motion work.

## Brand Personality

Professional and meticulous, with warmth expressed through restrained, high-quality interaction.

## Anti-references

Avoid cheap-looking effects, ornamental animation, conventional résumé templates, loud colors, and visibly generic portfolio patterns.

## Design Principles

- Let the work carry the visual authority.
- Use interaction to reward attention, never to obstruct reading.
- Make professional detail visible through typography, spacing, and motion.
- Keep every path from project evidence to contact information direct.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Keep content keyboard accessible and readable, with a reduced-motion experience for visitors who request it.

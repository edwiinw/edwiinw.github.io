# Design System

## Direction

A restrained midnight portfolio with a single cobalt signal color, precise typography, and tactile cursor-responsive depth.

## Color

- Canvas: `oklch(16% 0.025 260)`
- Surface: `oklch(20% 0.025 260)`
- Text: `oklch(96% 0.008 255)`
- Muted text: `oklch(74% 0.018 255)`
- Accent: `oklch(63% 0.21 258)`
- Hairline: `oklch(100% 0 0 / 0.14)`

## Typography

Use Manrope as a single deliberate family with strong scale contrast. Display text is compact and calm; body copy is open and highly readable.

## Layout

Full-viewport opening scene, generous negative space, asymmetric project rows, and a compact résumé timeline. Corners remain restrained at 14px maximum.

## Motion

Cursor-responsive portrait parallax, magnetic links, and soft section reveals. All motion is disabled or simplified under `prefers-reduced-motion`.
